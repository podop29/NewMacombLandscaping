Gutter Cleaning Services

Gutter Cleaning in Macomb, MI

Prevent Water Damage with Professional Gutter Maintenance

Clogged gutters can lead to roof leaks, fascia rot, foundation issues, and messy exterior staining. Macomb Landscaping Services provides thorough, safe, and reliable gutter cleaning for homes and businesses across Macomb, Shelby Township, Chesterfield, and nearby communities.

Our service removes debris, restores proper water flow, and helps extend the life of your roof and gutters—recommended at least twice per year (spring and late fall).

What Our Gutter Cleaning Includes

  • Hand removal of leaves, twigs, and roof debris from gutters
  • Complete downspout flushing to ensure clear drainage
  • Minor adjustments to loose hangers and visible pitch issues
  • Bagging and disposal of all debris—no mess left behind
  • Visual inspection for trouble spots (leaks, sagging, separations)

Add-ons available upon request: gutter whitening (exterior stain cleanup), leaf guard install, and small seam reseals.

Recommended Schedule

  • Spring Clean: Clear winter debris and early leaf buds to protect during heavy spring rains.
  • Late-Fall Clean: After most leaves drop, prep for freeze/thaw cycles and winter storms.

Underground Buried Gutter Drainage

Looking for a more permanent solution? We also install underground buried gutter drainage systems that connect your downspouts to buried PVC pipes, directing water safely away from your foundation. This eliminates surface runoff, prevents erosion and foundation issues, and keeps your landscaping clean and dry year-round.
